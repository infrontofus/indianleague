Welcome to Indian League Season 3 – 2025!

Get ready for the adrenaline-pumping action as one of India’s biggest and most prestigious state-level sports leagues returns, bigger and better than ever. Hosted in the vibrant state of Karnataka, this Olympic-style multi-sport extravaganza will bring together top athletes from across Indian states to compete in a thrilling lineup of sports — including cricket, football, athletics, and more. From May 24 to July 6, 2025, witness unmatched passion, talent, and sportsmanship as the Indian League ignites the spirit of unity and competition across the nation.
